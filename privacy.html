Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how SnapPet: Cat & Dog Collector handles information. SnapPet is designed to protect users and street animals: animal locations are not collected, shared, or written to our backend.

1. Information We Collect

SnapPet may collect the following information to operate the app:

  • Anonymous user identifier generated by Firebase Authentication.
  • Nickname and friend code chosen or assigned for social features.
  • Gameplay progress such as total pals, legendary count, fed count, level, XP, streaks, badges, and leaderboard scores.
  • Location-free public card metadata for social views, such as species, rarity, card name, and whether the pal was marked as fed.
  • Technical security signals needed for Firebase App Check and abuse prevention.
  • Crash diagnostics collected through Firebase Crashlytics, such as crash data needed to find and fix app stability issues.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect or share precise animal locations.
  • We do not write location fields to Firestore.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not use collected data for tracking across other companies' apps or websites.
  • We do not upload your captured pet photos to social features by default.

3. Camera, Photos, and Animal Detection

SnapPet uses the device camera so you can discover cats and dogs in real time. Captured photos are stored locally on your device for your collection unless you choose to export or share an image. On-device animal recognition can be used to confirm that a cat or dog is present.

4. Location

Location is optional. If the app offers a personal map feature, location stays on your device and is used only for your private experience. Friends, leaderboards, and backend services do not receive animal location data.

5. Social Features and Leaderboards

SnapPet uses Firebase to support anonymous accounts, friend codes, public collection views, and leaderboards. Other users may see your nickname, friend code, scores, and location-free public card metadata. They cannot see where an animal was found.

6. Feeding Verification and AI Services

SnapPet may use server-side services, including AI classification behind a Cloud Function, to verify feeding-related signals or creative optional features. These services are not used to change the deterministic identity of a card. API keys are not stored in the app client.

7. Data Sharing

We use service providers only to run core app functionality, including Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions, App Check, and Firebase Crashlytics for crash diagnostics. If advertising or optional paid features are introduced, this policy will be updated before those features are used in production.

8. App Store Privacy Label

In App Store Connect, SnapPet's privacy label is intended to match the app's current data use:

  • Identifiers: User ID, used for app functionality, not tracking.
  • Other Data: nickname, friend code, gameplay progress, and scores, used for app functionality, not tracking.
  • Diagnostics: Crash Data, collected through Firebase Crashlytics to improve app stability, not tracking.
  • Location: not collected by SnapPet backend services. Animal locations stay on device.
  • Tracking: no.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Gameplay and account data are kept while your anonymous account is active. You can request account deletion from inside the app. Deletion is intended to remove backend account data, local app data, and the Firebase Auth identity associated with the app account.

To delete your SnapPet account in the app:

  1. Open SnapPet.
  2. Go to Profile or Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion prompt.

After confirmation, SnapPet attempts to delete your backend account data, local app data, and anonymous Firebase Auth identity. If you cannot access the app or need help with deletion, contact sketchhero.team@gmail.com.

10. Children

SnapPet is not intended for children under the minimum age required by local law to use online services without parental consent. If you believe a child provided information without proper consent, contact us so we can delete it.

11. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

12. Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact sketchhero.team@gmail.com.

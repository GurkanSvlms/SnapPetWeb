Core loop

Find. Save. Feed. Share.

01

Live camera discovery

SnapPet uses the live camera to recognize cats and dogs. Photo import is not part of the core loop, keeping the game focused on real encounters.

02

Deterministic cards

The same animal moment creates the same game identity through a deterministic card engine: species, rarity, name, traits, and stats.

03

Albums and progress

Build personal albums, earn XP, keep streaks, unlock badges, and collect everything from common pals to legendary finds.

04

Feeding is rewarded

Feeding is a positive incentive, not a paywall. Fed pals can receive special badges and bonus progress while capture remains free.

Privacy promise

Built to protect animals, not expose them.

SnapPet is designed around a strict rule: animal locations do not leave the device. Friends and leaderboards are about collection progress, not maps of street animals.

No public animal map Location is optional and local-only. It is never written to Firestore.
No paid capture wall Capturing and collecting pals stays free. Monetization focuses on optional cosmetics and creative extras.
No photo sharing by default Photos stay on the device unless the user chooses to export or share an image.

Social without risk

Compete on kindness and collection skill.

Friends, public cards, and leaderboards

Add friends with a short code, compare collections, and climb leaderboards by total pals, legendary finds, and fed pals. Shared data is intentionally limited to nickname, friend code, scores, and location-free public card metadata.

  • Nickname
  • Friend code
  • Scores
  • Species
  • Rarity
  • Fed badge
